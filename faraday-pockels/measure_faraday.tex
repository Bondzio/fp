\subsection{Measurement of $2\epsilon$}

\begin{center}
 The Measurement of the angles for the $2\epsilon$ yield:
\begin{align}
    \alpha_0 = 170 \pm 5, \quad
    \alpha_1 = 8   \pm 3 \\
    2\epsilon = \alpha_0 - \alpha_1 = 162.0 \pm 6   
\end{align}
\end{center}
